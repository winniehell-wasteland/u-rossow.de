Dr. Uwe Rossow

experimental physicist in solid-state physics (semiconductors)

Currently, I am working as a senior scientist (Akademischer Rat) at TU Braunschweig. I am mainly involved in the characterization and growth of semiconductor layers and nanostructures and the optical response of surfaces. My speciality is the optical characterization with the polarization sensitive methods spectroscopic ellipsometry (SE) and reflectance difference spectroscopy (RDS, RAS). I also worked with other classical surface spectroscopies such as photoemission (XPS) or Auger electron spectroscopy (AES).
